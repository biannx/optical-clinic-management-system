Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What kinds of products and services do we sell?
    We mainly sell eyeglasses in different styles, which you will see on the Products page. You may schedule an online appointment on the Set Appointment page.
  2. What eyeglasses styles can I choose?
    You may select your eyeglass style with the width, material, hinge, and finish in the filtering selection on the Product page. You may choose the lens type with normal or UV Lens of your selected product.
  3. Is it possible to order more than one eyeglass each order?
    You can order as many glasses as you want. After adding a pair of glasses to your Shopping Cart, click the Back to Shopping button at the upper right of the Shopping Cart page. Then order the next eyeglass. Repeat until you have all the glasses you want to order.
  4. Do I need an account to schedule an appointment?
    Yes, you will need an account on our website to be able to schedule an appointment and order products.
  5. Do you offer Shipping of orders?
    No. The Shipping of online orders is not yet available in the system.
  6. Do you accept online payment?
    Unfortunately, we cannot accept online payment at this time. We are working on changing this as a matter of priority.
  7. I have a concern, who do I contact?
    You can find the contact details at the bottom of the website, which includes email and a contact number.
  8. I don't know what my eye grade is. What should I do before buying?
    We assist customers in knowing their eye grades to provide the proper eyeglasses for you.
  9. Is it safe to put my credit card number on this website?
    Yes. We don't store your credit card information that our payment processor handles. We will never share your personal information with anyone else.
